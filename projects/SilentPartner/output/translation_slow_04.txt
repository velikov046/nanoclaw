## NOTES

- "le ne" is rendered descriptively as "the 'ne' particle" rather than translated; it is a grammatical label with no English equivalent and was kept in quotes throughout.
- "ciné" and "resto" were glossed with their full forms in parentheses on first use, then left as-is to match the register of the lesson (the truncation is the point being taught).
- "patron" was rendered as "boss" rather than "owner"; context suggested an employment relationship, but either reading is plausible.
