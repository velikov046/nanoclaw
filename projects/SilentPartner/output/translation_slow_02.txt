## NOTES

- "در پناه خدا" is a religious farewell idiom; rendered as "in God's protection" rather than the more generic "goodbye" to preserve register.
- "خیلی شما باشید" is a colloquial Farsi closing; closest natural English equivalent is "be well, all of you" -- no single word captures it.
- "حسا" and "طالعون" are phonetically transcribed and may be names with alternate spellings; flagged as uncertain in the glossary.
