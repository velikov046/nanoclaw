## SESSION CONTEXT
Persian (Farsi) to English. Informal gathering or group activity: participants introducing themselves and receiving brief instructions from an organiser.

## GLOSSARY

| Source term | Target rendering |
|---|---|
| بهار | Bahar |
| در پناه خدا | in God's protection |
| سیمون | Seymour / Simon |
| کامران | Kamran |
| خدا | God |
| Seymour John | Seymour John (goes by Seymour) |

## NOTES

- "سیمون" is ambiguous between Simon and Seymour; the glossary records both since the full name "Seymour John" clarifies it in context.
- "در پناه خدا" is a conventional Persian farewell/blessing; rendered idiomatically rather than literally.
