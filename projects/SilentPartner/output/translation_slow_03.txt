## SESSION CONTEXT

Persian (Farsi) to English. A group introduction session where participants take turns greeting and sharing brief personal details, including profession and recent activities. Religious blessing exchanges are common throughout.

## NOTES

- "دستازی" is an idiomatic blessing directed at someone's hands after they've done skilled work; rendered as "bless your hands" to preserve the warmth, though English has no direct equivalent.
- "ناخن اتاک کردم" is a loanword construction (from "attack" / acrylic nail application); "got my nails done" captures the meaning without the hybrid phrasing.
- Speaker identities for S4 and the distinction between S5/Mehran and Mrs. Sami remain ambiguous from context alone.
