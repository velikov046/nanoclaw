## NOTES

- "Tu m'étonnes" is rendered as "tell me about it" rather than the literal "you astonish me" -- the literal reading is false-friend territory; the idiomatic sense is strong agreement or commiseration.
- "ma foule" as "my crowd" preserves the informal, affectionate address to the audience without over-translating to "my fans" or "my followers."
- "de garde" kept as "on call" throughout; context is hospital scheduling, so the medical register is appropriate.
